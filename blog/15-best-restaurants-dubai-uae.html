15 Best Restaurants in Dubai, UAE

Curated guide to Dubai's finest dining establishments, featuring diverse cuisines and exceptional culinary experiences.

Mohamad Ahmad Luxury Real Estate Expert • Associate Partner at M R One Properties
10 min read Share
Dubai's culinary scene offers world-class dining experiences across diverse cuisines

Dubai's culinary scene reflects its international character, offering world-class dining experiences across diverse cuisines. This carefully curated list features 15 exceptional restaurants that define Dubai's gastronomic landscape.

Share this article:

Fine Dining Excellence

Dubai's fine dining scene includes Michelin-starred establishments and celebrity chef restaurants. Venues like Nobu, Zuma, and Pierchic offer exceptional culinary experiences with stunning ambiance.

1. Nobu Dubai

Location: Atlantis The Royal
Cuisine: Japanese-Peruvian Fusion
World-renowned chef Nobu Matsuhisa's signature dishes with stunning views of the Arabian Gulf.

2. Zuma Dubai

Location: Gate Village, DIFC
Cuisine: Contemporary Japanese
Stylish izakaya-style dining with premium sushi, robatayaki, and sake selection.

3. Pierchic

Location: Al Qasr, Madinat Jumeirah
Cuisine: Seafood
Romantic overwater restaurant specializing in fresh seafood and Mediterranean cuisine.

4. La Petite Maison

Location: Gate Village, DIFC
Cuisine: French Mediterranean
Elegant French cuisine with Mediterranean influences and impeccable service.

5. Ossiano

Location: Atlantis The Palm
Cuisine: Contemporary European
Underwater dining experience with floor-to-ceiling aquarium views and innovative cuisine.

Cultural Cuisine

Authentic Middle Eastern and Emirati restaurants preserve traditional flavors while incorporating modern techniques. Al Hadheerah and Sikka Cafe offer genuine regional dining experiences.

6. Al Hadheerah

Location: Al Sahra Desert Resort
Cuisine: Emirati & Arabian
Desert dining experience with traditional entertainment and authentic regional cuisine.

7. Sikka Cafe

Location: City Walk
Cuisine: Modern Emirati
Contemporary take on Emirati classics in a stylish, culturally-inspired setting.

8. Al Fanar Restaurant & Cafe

Location: Multiple locations
Cuisine: Traditional Emirati
Authentic Emirati dishes served in a nostalgic setting celebrating local heritage.

International Flavors

Dubai's cosmopolitan nature is reflected in its diverse international cuisine options. From Italian at Roberto's to Japanese at Tomo, the city offers authentic global dining experiences.

9. Roberto's

Location: Jumeirah Al Qasr
Cuisine: Italian
Authentic Italian cuisine with handmade pasta and wood-fired pizzas in an elegant setting.

10. Tomo

Location: Raffles Dubai
Cuisine: Japanese
Traditional Japanese cuisine with premium ingredients and meticulous preparation techniques.

11. Indego by Vineet

Location: Grosvenor House Dubai
Cuisine: Modern Indian
Award-winning chef Vineet Bhatia's contemporary take on Indian flavors and spices.

Casual Dining Gems

Beyond fine dining, Dubai offers exceptional casual restaurants that deliver quality food in relaxed settings. These venues provide excellent value and consistent quality for regular dining.

12. Tom & Serg

Location: Al Quoz & City Walk
Cuisine: International Cafe
Industrial-chic cafe serving exceptional coffee, creative brunch dishes, and healthy options.

13. The Sum of Us

Location: Jumeirah
Cuisine: Modern Australian
Neighborhood gem offering fresh, seasonal dishes with excellent coffee and relaxed atmosphere.

14. Common Grounds

Location: Alserkal Avenue
Cuisine: International Bistro
Hip warehouse setting with creative menu, specialty coffee, and artisanal cocktails.

15. Bu Qtair

Location: Jumeirah Beach Road
Cuisine: Fresh Seafood
No-frills local institution serving the freshest grilled fish and prawns by the beach.

Dining Tips for Dubai

  • Reservations: Book in advance for popular restaurants, especially during weekends and holidays
  • Dress Code: Many fine dining establishments have smart casual to formal dress codes
  • Timing: Lunch is typically 12:30-3pm, dinner from 7pm onwards
  • Alcohol: Only licensed restaurants serve alcohol; many venues offer excellent mocktails
  • Cuisine Diversity: Explore beyond hotel restaurants - Dubai's neighborhoods have hidden culinary gems

Conclusion

Dubai's restaurant scene continues to evolve, attracting world-renowned chefs and innovative concepts. The city's dining landscape offers something exceptional for every palate and occasion.
